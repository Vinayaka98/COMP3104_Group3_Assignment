1. The word “DevOps” was coined in 2009 by Patrick Debois, who became one of its gurus.

2. DevOps is all about the unification and automation of processes, and DevOps engineers are instrumental in combining code, application maintenance, and application management. All of these tasks rely on understanding not only development life cycles, but DevOps culture, and its philosophy, practices, and tools.
